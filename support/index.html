Support

Yomm is a small app for letting people you choose know they are on your mind. If something is not working, or something in Yomm feels wrong, we can help.

Getting help

Tell us what happened, the device you are using, and the best way to reply. If you are signed in, you can also use the Feedback button in Yomm.

Invites and App Clip

Yomm invitations are single-use and expire. If a friend code is not available anymore, ask your friend to send a new invite. If you used the App Clip to save an invite, install Yomm, sign in with Apple, complete your profile, and confirm the saved connection in the full app.

Reporting a profile or account

If a profile photo or account feels unsafe, abusive, impersonating, or otherwise wrong, remove the connection in Yomm and let us know. Yomm may remove photos or accounts that misuse the service.

Privacy and account deletion

You can read the Privacy Policy at any time. You can permanently delete your account from the avatar menu in Yomm. Include privacy or account-data requests when you contact us.
